Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

Formulist ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") respects the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use the Formulist mobile application.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Formulist does not directly collect any personal information from its users. No account creation is required.

Locally Stored Data (Stays on Your Device):
- Your favorite formulas
- App settings (language, screen brightness, theme)
- Password you set for privacy mode (stored in your device's secure storage)

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Formulist uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob: Used to display in-app advertisements. AdMob may collect information such as advertising identifier (IDFA/AAID), IP address, device information, and in-app interactions to serve personalized ads.
Learn more: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Firebase: Used to monitor app performance and report errors.
Learn more: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Data collection by AdMob is subject to age restrictions defined by Apple App Store and Google Play.

4. PERMISSIONS

Formulist may require the following permissions:
- Internet access (only to display ads)
- Device information (for ad optimization)

5. DATA DELETION

When you uninstall the App from your device, all locally stored data is deleted.

6. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

This policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be announced within the App.
